6/8: (18 minutes) Called again as service was still out. They said they would escalate the issue and get back in touch with me.

6/9: (15 minutes) No contact was made by anyone at Comcast. UPS tried to deliver something from Comcast. I rejected the delivery as I had not ordered anything. I called back as the service was still down. Spoke with Hashim and was told, yes, my account had been changed to add new services. I made sure that they rolled back any changes made to my account.

6/10: (Call one, 10 minutes, hung up on… Call two, 30 minutes) Called at 9:50 to see where the technician is. Told George the whole story… He comes back on the line and asks if I can be around on hold until tomorrow for an appointment.
